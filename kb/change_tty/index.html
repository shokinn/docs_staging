    Change TTY¶

    With Keybindings¶

    From X to TTY¶

    Use: CTRL + ALT + F{1..n}

    From TTY to X¶

    Use: ALT + F{F1..n}

    With a shell command on a TTY¶

    Use:

    chvt 1..n
